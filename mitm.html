Murder In The Mansion

A detective game written in blood... and Python.

Overview

The Case: John Wallace has been murdered. You, Detective, must uncover the truth. Read files. Interrogate suspects. Follow the timelines. But beware... the evidence changes as you dig deeper.

In-Depth Case File

Murder in the Mansion isn't just a game—it's a mental chess match against the killer, the clock, and even time itself!

Gameplay Flow

  • Classic detective interface: music, static imagery, and a main suspect portrait.
  • Branching timelines and evidence that changes as you investigate.
  • Asynchronous text-based puzzles and clues.

Tools at Your Disposal

  • Evidence Board: Organize and review all clues and evidence.
  • Interrogation Interface: Question suspects and witness statements.
  • Click Reconstruction: See evidence in timelines.
  • Arrest/Warrant Submission: Choose wisely!
  • Help System: Get tips if you’re stuck.

Endgame & Consequences

  • Submit your verdict and see the truth dramatically revealed.
  • Multiple endings based on your investigation and choices.

How It Works

  • Built in Python with Pygame
  • Custom timeline engine
  • Multiple endings and logic-driven clues

Under the Hood

  • Python 3.10
  • Pygame
  • Custom timeline DSL
  • Modular file loading
