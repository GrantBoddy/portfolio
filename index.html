About Me

I was built to build. To create. To fix. To take things apart, understand them, and then rebuild them better than before. In the technology frontier, I found the perfect combination: being able to use logic that anchors me, and then being able to also apply my creativity that sets me free. Here, code isn’t just syntax..... it’s potential.

My path wasn’t the traditional academic route, no, I definitely took the scenic route to get here. After high school, I spent years in the workforce. Starting with tough, hands-on manual labor jobs. These jobs taught me how to always be consistent, how to lead with discipline, how to show up with grit, how to stay resilient under pressure, how to take initiative, how to always be reliable, and above all, how to outwork everyone around me, day after day. They taught me how to show up early, stay late, and take pride in doing things the right way, even when no one's watching. I credit those roles with gifting me my unstoppable and unparalleled work ethic, something I carry into every single project I create.

But no matter where I worked, the same thing always happened: people noticed me. They noticed how I thought differently, how I always had the most output, how I worked harder, faster, smarter, and especially how I always solved problems. I was often moved into tech-focused roles where I was given the systems. Asked to “figure it out.” Whether it was on the job or at a family gathering, I was always the one fixing computers, solving problems, bridging the gap between people and technology.

That instinct eventually became intention. I decided to lean all the way in and go back to school where I earned my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. While leading the majority of my classes, I also dove headfirst into professional certifications, earning over 240 professional industry certificates across the entire tech landscape.

I wanted to learn everything, so I did. From diving into cybersecurity with Wireshark and Python, to exploring QA automation with Playwright, to building dashboards with SQL, Excel, and Power BI, and creating full-stack applications with JavaScript, React, and Node.js. I’ve even built ML models with TensorFlow and scikit-learn just to see how they work.

I don’t just work hard..... I work purposefully. I bring extreme energy, drive, and curiosity to every task and always throw 110% of myself into the work, not just because I have to, but because I want to.
I don't just want to code, I want to build, to create, to improve, to make an impact. That collision of ambition, curiosity, and discipline is not just where I belong...... it's where I thrive.
